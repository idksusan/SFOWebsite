About our club

The Student Film Organization meets every Thursday from 7 PM to 8 PM, usually in 110 Sackett. Our activities vary from meeting to meeting, but everything is always done in the interest of spreading love for film all across campus and bringing the spotlight to the film community here in State College. Examples of things we've done in the past include short film competitions (such as the 4-Day Film Fest), workshops on various film techniques, outings to nearby screenings, and fun film-related games.
